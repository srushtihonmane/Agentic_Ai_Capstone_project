NimbusPay Careers — Job ID 4471

Software Engineering Intern (Payments Platform)
Location: Bengaluru, India (Hybrid — 3 days in office)
Duration: 6 months | Stipend: INR 80,000/month
Application deadline: 21 July 2026

About NimbusPay
NimbusPay processes UPI and card payments for 40,000+ online merchants. Our platform team builds the APIs, ledgers, and reconciliation systems that move money reliably at scale.

What you will do
- Design and ship backend services in Python (FastAPI) or Java (Spring Boot) for the merchant payments API.
- Write and optimize SQL against PostgreSQL for transaction reporting workloads.
- Build internal dashboards to monitor payment success rates and latency.
- Participate in code reviews and write unit/integration tests for everything you ship.
- Work with senior engineers on incident retrospectives and reliability improvements.

Minimum qualifications
- Pursuing a B.Tech/B.E. in CS or related field, graduating 2026 or 2027.
- Strong grasp of data structures, algorithms, and REST API design.
- Proficiency in Python or Java.
- Hands-on experience with SQL and relational databases (PostgreSQL/MySQL).
- Comfortable with Git-based workflows.

Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Docker and CI/CD pipelines.
- Familiarity with AWS (EC2, S3, RDS) or any major cloud.
- Exposure to Kubernetes.
- A deployed side project or open-source contributions.
- Prior internship experience at a product company.

How to apply
Apply through the NimbusPay careers portal with your resume and a short note on a system you have built. Shortlisted candidates complete one online assessment followed by two technical interviews.
